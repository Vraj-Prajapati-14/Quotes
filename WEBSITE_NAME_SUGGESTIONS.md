## Domain Selection Tips

- Choose a .com domain for best credibility
- Keep it short (under 15 characters if possible)
- Include primary keywords (quotes, shayari, status)
- Make it easy to remember and type
- Avoid hyphens if possible
- Check domain availability before finalizing

## Branding Considerations

- The name should reflect your content (quotes, shayari, status)
- Consider your target audience (Hindi/English speakers, WhatsApp users)
- Think about social media handles availability
- Ensure the name is pronounceable and memorable
- Consider future expansion possibilities
